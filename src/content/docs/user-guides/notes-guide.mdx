## Overview

The **Notes** feature lets you keep private notes tied to your account. Notes support rich content, revision history, soft delete with a trash bin, and restore from previous versions.

**Route**: `/dashboard/notes`

## Creating a Note

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Notes** (`/dashboard/notes`)
2. Click **New Note**
3. Enter a **title** (required)
4. Add your note content
5. Save the note

Notes are private to your account. Admins and superusers cannot read your note content unless a permission is explicitly granted.

## Editing a Note

1. Open the note from the list
2. Click **Edit**
3. Update the title or content
4. Save your changes

Each save creates a new **revision**. The previous version is kept so you can restore it later.

## Revision History

Every note keeps a history of changes.

- Click the expand icon on a note to see its revisions
- Each revision shows the date and time it was saved
- Click **Restore** on a revision to roll the note back to that version
- A configurable revision limit prevents unlimited history growth

## Soft Delete and Trash Bin

If you delete a note, it is not removed permanently right away.

1. Click **Delete** on a note
2. The note moves to the **Trash Bin**
3. From the trash bin you can:
   - **Restore** the note to your active notes
   - **Delete Permanently** to remove it forever

This protects against accidental deletion.

## Notes Widget on Dashboard

A **Notes Widget** can appear on patron dashboards, showing a quick preview of your most recent notes and a shortcut to create a new one.

## For Admins

Administrative users can manage notes through the system if granted permission:

- View notes list for oversight
- Moderate content when required by policy
- Manage trash bin cleanup

## Tips

- Use a clear title so notes are easy to find later
- Keep revision history in mind when making large edits
- Empty the trash bin periodically if you no longer need deleted notes
