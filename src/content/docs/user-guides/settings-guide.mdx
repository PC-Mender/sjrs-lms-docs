## Overview

The Settings page is your central hub for managing account preferences, notification settings, security options, and more. All roles access settings from the same unified route.

**Route**: `/dashboard/settings`

## Accessing Settings

1. Click your **profile avatar** in the top-right corner
2. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu
3. Or navigate directly to `/dashboard/settings`

## Settings Sections

### General Settings

- **Display name** — how your name appears to other users
- **Profile picture** — upload or change your avatar
- **Language preference** — interface language (if multilingual is enabled)
- **Timezone** — affects date/time display throughout the app

### Notification Preferences

**Route**: `/dashboard/notification-preferences`

1. Toggle which notification types you receive:
   - Due date reminders
   - Overdue alerts
   - Reservation-ready notifications
   - Badge awards
   - Penalty notifications
   - Announcements
2. Choose delivery channels:
   - **In-app** — notifications appear in the notification center
   - **Email** — notifications sent to your registered email
3. Click **Save** to update preferences

### Security Settings

#### Change Password

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security** tab
2. Enter your **current password**
3. Enter your **new password** (minimum 8 characters)
4. Confirm the new password
5. Click **Change Password**

#### Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

**Route**: `/dashboard/mfa-settings`

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security** → **Two-Factor Authentication**
2. Click **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**
3. Choose your method:
   - **Authenticator App** — scan QR code with Google Authenticator or Authy
   - **Email** — receive OTP codes via email
   - **Passkey** — register your device (FaceID, TouchID) for passwordless login
4. Save your **backup codes** in a safe place
5. Use a backup code if you lose access to your primary MFA method

<Aside type="danger" title="Backup Codes">
Store your backup codes securely. If you lose access to your MFA device and don't have backup codes, you'll need to contact a superuser to reset MFA.
</Aside>

#### Session Management

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security** → **Active Sessions**
2. View all devices currently logged into your account
3. See device name, IP address, location, and last active time
4. Click **Revoke** to end any session you don't recognize

### Privacy Settings

- **Profile visibility** — control who can see your profile information
- **Activity status** — show/hide your online status
- **Reading history** — enable/disable tracking of your reading activity

## API Reference

| Method | Endpoint | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| GET | `/api/v1/user/profile` | Get current user profile |
| PUT | `/api/v1/user/profile` | Update profile settings |
| GET | `/api/v1/user/preferences` | Get notification preferences |
| PUT | `/api/v1/user/preferences` | Update notification preferences |
| POST | `/api/v1/auth/change-password` | Change password |
| GET | `/api/v1/auth/sessions` | List active sessions |
| DELETE | `/api/v1/auth/sessions/:id` | Revoke a session |
| POST | `/api/v1/auth/mfa/enroll` | Enable MFA |
| POST | `/api/v1/auth/mfa/disable` | Disable MFA |
