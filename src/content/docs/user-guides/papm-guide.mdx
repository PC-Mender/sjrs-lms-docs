## Overview

**PAPM** stands for **Private Account Payment Module**. It is used to record and request reimbursement of payments made from a private account on behalf of the institution.

**Route**: `/dashboard/papm`

## Who Can Use PAPM

- Students, professors, staff, and guests with permission to create PAPM submissions
- Administrative roles can review, approve, or reject submissions

## Creating a PAPM Submission

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** → **PAPM** (`/dashboard/papm`)
2. Click **Create PAPM**
3. Complete the form:
   - **Payment details** — amount, date, purpose, and description
   - **Drawee information** — one or more people on whose behalf the payment was made
   - **Drawee metadata** — registration number, year of study, and department where applicable
4. Save as a **draft** or **submit** for approval

## Drawees

A drawee is the person benefiting from the payment.

- You can add multiple drawees to a single PAPM form
- Drafts may be saved without drawees and completed later
- Once submitted, the drawee list is locked

## Viewing and Printing

The PAPM list shows:

- Submission reference
- Date and amount
- Drawee names
- Status
- Quick actions: view, edit (draft only), print

Clicking a submission opens a read-only modal view. Click **Print** to generate a printable version with watermark.

## Finalizing a Submission

When you are ready to submit:

1. Open the draft
2. Confirm all drawee details are correct
3. Click **Finalize & Submit**
4. The form will lock and enter the approval workflow

## For Approvers

Administrative users can:

- View all PAPM submissions
- Filter by status, amount, or date
- Approve or reject with comments
- Track submission history

## Important Notes

- Submitted PAPM forms cannot be edited
- Keep supporting receipts or documents for verification
- Use the print preview to review before final submission
