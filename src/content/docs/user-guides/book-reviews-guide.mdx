## Overview

Book reviews let users share their opinions about books they've read. Students and regular users see only their own reviews. Librarians and admins can moderate all reviews.

**Route**: `/dashboard/book-reviews`

## Viewing Reviews

### As a Regular User

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** → **Book Reviews** (`/dashboard/book-reviews`)
2. You'll see a table of your reviews with book title, rating, and status
3. The user column is hidden since you only see your own reviews

### As a Librarian/Admin/Superuser

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** → **Book Reviews**
2. The table shows all reviews from all users, with user columns visible
3. Use the **search bar** to find reviews by book title or user

## Writing a Review

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Book Reviews**
2. Click **Create Review**
3. Select the book you want to review
4. Provide a **rating** (1–5 stars)
5. Write your **review text**
6. Click **Submit** to publish

## Editing Your Review

1. Find your review in the list
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon)
3. Update the rating or review text
4. Click **Update** to save

## Deleting Your Review

1. Find your review in the list
2. Click the **Delete** button (trash icon)
3. Confirm the deletion

## For Librarians, Admins & Superusers

### Moderating Reviews

Staff can:
- **View** all reviews from all users
- **Edit** any review (to remove inappropriate content)
- **Delete** any review

### Review Statuses

Reviews may have moderation statuses:
- **Published** — visible to all users
- **Pending** — awaiting moderation
- **Hidden** — hidden by staff for policy violations

## API Reference

| Method | Endpoint | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| GET | `/api/v1/book-reviews` | List reviews (user-scoped or all) |
| GET | `/api/v1/book-reviews/:id` | Get review details |
| POST | `/api/v1/book-reviews` | Create a review |
| PUT | `/api/v1/book-reviews/:id` | Update a review |
| DELETE | `/api/v1/book-reviews/:id` | Delete a review |
