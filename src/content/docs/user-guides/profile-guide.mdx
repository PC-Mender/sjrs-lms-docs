## Overview

Your profile contains your personal information, role, and account status. Keeping your profile complete and up-to-date ensures smooth library operations and proper access to features.

**Route**: `/dashboard/profile`

## Viewing Your Profile

1. Click your **profile avatar** in the top-right corner
2. Select **My Profile** from the dropdown
3. Or navigate directly to `/dashboard/profile`

Your profile page displays:
- **Personal information** — name, email, phone, date of birth
- **Role and permissions** — your assigned role(s) and what you can access
- **Account status** — active, pending, suspended
- **Member details** — student ID, department, year (for students)
- **Activity summary** — books borrowed, reservations, reviews, badges

## Completing Your Profile

New users must complete their profile after email confirmation:

1. After registering and confirming your email, you'll be redirected to the **Profile Completion** page (`/dashboard/profile-completion`)
2. Fill in required fields:
   - **First name** and **last name**
   - **Phone number**
   - **Date of birth**
   - Role-specific fields (e.g., student ID for students, department for professors)
3. Click **Submit** to complete your profile
4. Your account moves to "Pending Approval" status
5. A librarian or admin will review and approve your account

<Aside type="note" title="Approval Required">
After completing your profile, your account must be approved by library staff before you can access the full system. You'll receive a notification when your account is approved.
</Aside>

## Editing Your Profile

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Profile** (`/dashboard/profile`)
2. Click **Edit Profile**
3. Update your personal information
4. Click **Save** to update

### What You Can Edit

- First name, last name
- Phone number
- Date of birth
- Profile picture
- Department/section (if applicable)

### What You Cannot Edit

- **Email address** — contact a superuser to change your email
- **Role** — assigned by superusers only
- **Account status** — managed by admins/superusers

## Profile Statuses

| Status | Description | What You Can Do |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| Pending Email Confirmation | Just registered, email not yet confirmed | Confirm email to proceed |
| Profile Incomplete | Email confirmed but profile not filled | Complete profile to proceed |
| Pending Approval | Profile complete, awaiting staff review | Wait for approval notification |
| Active | Account approved and in good standing | Full system access |
| Suspended | Account suspended by staff | Contact library staff |

## Switching Roles

If you have multiple roles (e.g., Student and Library Assistant):

1. Click your **profile avatar** in the top-right
2. Select **Switch Role**
3. Choose the role you want to use
4. The dashboard updates to show role-appropriate features

## API Reference

| Method | Endpoint | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| GET | `/api/v1/user/profile` | Get current user profile |
| PUT | `/api/v1/user/profile` | Update profile |
| POST | `/api/v1/auth/profile-completion` | Complete profile (new users) |
| GET | `/api/v1/auth/check-status` | Check account status |
