## Account & Security

### **How do I reset my password?**
If you have forgotten your password or need to change it:
1. Go to the **Login Page**.
2. Click the **"Forgot Password?"** link.
3. Enter your registered **email address**.
4. Check your email for a **password reset link**.
5. Click the link and enter your new password.
    *   *Note: Passwords must be at least 8 characters long.*

### **How do I troubleshoot login issues?**
If you cannot log in:
1. **Check Credentials**: Ensure your email and password are correct.
2. **Session Limit**: You may have too many active sessions. The system limits concurrent sessions for security. Try logging out from other devices.
3. **Account Lockout**: After 5 failed attempts, your account may be temporarily locked. Wait 30 minutes and try again.
4. **Browser Issues**: Clear your browser cache or try a different browser.
5. **Contact Support**: If the issue persists, contact a Librarian or Admin.

### **How do I view my login history?**
1. Open your **Profile Menu** (top right).
2. Select **"Session Details"**.
3. Review **Session History** for device, IP address, location, and start/end times.

### **How do I secure my account with MFA?**
1. Open your **Profile Menu** (top right).
2. Select **"Two-Factor Authentication"**.
3. Click **"Enable Two-Factor Authentication"**.
4. Choose your method:
    *   **Authenticator App**: Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator or Authy.
    *   **Email**: Verify your email address for OTPs.
    *   **Passkey**: Register your device (FaceID, TouchID) for passwordless login.
5. Save your **Backup Codes** in a safe place.

### **How do I manage my active sessions?**
If you suspect unauthorized access or forgot to logout:
1. Open your **Profile Menu** (top right).
2. Select **"Session Details"**.
3. Review the list of devices logged into your account.
4. Click **"Revoke"** next to any session you want to end immediately.

## Account & Profile

### **How do I switch between my roles?**
If you have multiple roles (e.g., Student and Library Assistant):
1. Click your **Profile Avatar** in the top right.
2. Select **"Switch Role"**.
3. Choose the role you want to use. The dashboard will update automatically.

### **How do I change my notification settings?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Settings** → **Notification Preferences** (`/dashboard/notification-preferences`).
2. Toggle the switches for **Email** and **In-App** notifications.
3. Customize alerts for **Loans**, **Orders**, and **System** updates.
4. Click **"Save Preferences"**.
    *   *See [Settings Guide](/user-guides/settings-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I update my profile?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Profile** (`/dashboard/profile`).
2. Click **Edit Profile**.
3. Update your details (Phone, Date of Birth, etc.).
4. Click **Save**.
    *   *Note: Email and Role cannot be changed — contact a superuser.*
    *   *See [Profile Guide](/user-guides/profile-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I pay a fine?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Penalties** (`/dashboard/penalties`).
2. Find the penalty with status "Pending".
3. Click **Pay**.
4. Complete the payment via **UPI**.
    *   *See [Penalties Guide](/user-guides/penalties-guide) for full details.*

## My Library (End Users)

### **How do I borrow a book?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Catalog** (`/dashboard/catalog`).
2. Search or filter to find the book.
3. Click the book card to open details.
4. Click **Reserve** to place a request.
5. Your loan will appear in **Dashboard** → **Loans** with status "Pending".
    *   *See [Catalog Guide](/user-guides/catalog-guide) and [Loans Guide](/user-guides/loans-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I reserve a book that's unavailable?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Catalog** (`/dashboard/catalog`).
2. Find the book (filter by Availability: "Unavailable").
3. Click the book card and click **Reserve**.
4. Or go to **Dashboard** → **Reservations** (`/dashboard/reservations`), click **Create Reservation**, and select a book.
5. You'll join the queue — check the **Position in Queue** column.
    *   *See [Reservations Guide](/user-guides/reservations-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I read a digital book?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Digital Resources** (`/dashboard/digital-resources`).
2. Click a resource to open the detail page.
3. Click **Read Now** to open the built-in reader.
4. Your progress is saved automatically — resume from where you left off.
    *   *See [Digital Resources Guide](/user-guides/digital-resources-guide) for full details.*

## Academic & Community

### **How do I generate a citation?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Citation Generator** (`/dashboard/citation-generator`).
2. Select the **source type** (book, journal article, etc.).
3. Search for the resource or enter details manually.
4. Choose the **citation style** (APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard, IEEE).
5. Click **Copy** to copy the generated citation.
    *   *See [Citation Generator Guide](/user-guides/citation-generator-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I check for plagiarism?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Plagiarism Checker** (`/dashboard/plagiarism-checker`).
2. Paste text or upload a file (.txt, .docx, .pdf).
3. Click **Check for Plagiarism**.
4. Review the **originality score** and **matched sources**.
    *   *See [Plagiarism Checker Guide](/user-guides/plagiarism-checker-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I write a book review?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Book Reviews** (`/dashboard/book-reviews`).
2. Click **Create Review**.
3. Select the book, provide a **rating** (1–5 stars), and write your review.
4. Click **Submit**.
    *   *See [Book Reviews Guide](/user-guides/book-reviews-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I manage bookmarks?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Bookmarks** (`/dashboard/bookmarks`).
2. **Add**: Click **Create Bookmark**, enter the title/URL, and save.
3. **Edit**: Click the edit icon to update.
4. **Delete**: Click the trash icon and confirm.
    *   *See [Bookmarks Guide](/user-guides/bookmarks-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I view my badges?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Badges** (`/dashboard/badges`).
2. Switch to the **My Badges** tab to see earned badges.
3. Switch to **Badge Catalog** to see all available badges and their criteria.
    *   *See [Badges Guide](/user-guides/badges-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I browse journal articles?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Journal Articles** (`/dashboard/articles`).
2. Use the search bar to find articles by title, author, or keyword.
3. Filter by journal, section, or publication date.
4. Click an article to view title, authors, abstract, and full text (if available).
    *   *See [Journals & Magazines Guide](/user-guides/journals-guide) for full details.*

## Book Management (Librarians/Admins)

### **How do I add a new book?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Books** (`/dashboard/books`).
2. Click **Create Book**.
3. Enter the book details (Title, Author, ISBN, etc.).
4. Click **Save** to create the book record.
    *   *Requires `books:create` permission (librarian, admin, superuser).*

### **How do I bulk import books?**
To add multiple books at once using a CSV file:
1. Navigate to **"Book Management"**.
2. Click the **"Import"** or **"Bulk Upload"** button.
3. Download the **CSV Template** if needed.
4. Upload your filled CSV file.
5. The system will process the file and report any errors or duplicates.

### **How do I "restore" a lost book?**
If a book copy was previously marked as "Lost" or "Damaged" but has been found or repaired:
1. Go to the **Book Details** page for the specific book.
2. Locate the **"Copies"** section.
3. Find the specific copy (by Copy Number) marked as `lost` or `damaged`.
4. Click the **"Edit"** icon/button for that copy.
5. Change the **Status** dropdown back to **`available`**.
6. Save your changes. The book is now back in circulation.

### **How do I weed (delete) books?**
To remove books from the catalog permanently (weeding):
1. Go to the **"Books"** management page.
2. Select the book(s) you wish to remove.
3. Click the **"Delete"** or **"Trash"** icon.
4. **Confirm** the action.
    *   *Note: Books with active loans cannot be deleted until the loans are closed.*

## Circulation (Loans & Returns)

### **How do I check out a book to a student?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Loans** (`/dashboard/loans`).
2. Click **Create Loan**.
3. Select the **User** and **Book Copy** from the dropdowns.
4. Set the **Due Date**.
5. Click **Create Loan**.
    *   *See [Loans Guide](/user-guides/loans-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I return a book?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Loans** (`/dashboard/loans`).
2. Find the loan with status "Active" or "Overdue".
3. Click the **Return** button (rollback arrow icon).
4. Confirm in the dialog.
    *   *See [Loans Guide](/user-guides/loans-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I renew a loan?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Loans** (`/dashboard/loans`).
2. Find an active loan within 3 days of its due date.
3. Click the **Renew** button (circular arrow icon).
4. A renewal request is sent to staff for approval.
    *   *See [Loans Guide](/user-guides/loans-guide) for full details.*

## System & Config (Admin)

### **How do I configure institution settings?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Institution Settings** (`/dashboard/institution-settings`).
2. Select the tab you want to configure:
    *   **General**: Institution name, address, contact info.
    *   **UPI Payments**: UPI ID, payee name, merchant code.
    *   **Policies**: Borrow period, renewal period, max reservations, fine rules.
3. Click **Save**.
    *   *Requires admin or superuser role. See [Institution Settings Guide](/user-guides/institution-settings-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I view system logs?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **System Logs** (`/dashboard/system-logs`).
2. Use the filters to search by **Action Type**, **Table Name**, **Date Range**, or **User ID**.
3. Click **Export** to download logs as CSV.
    *   *See [System Logs Guide](/user-guides/system-logs-user-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I manage users?**
1. Go to the **Admin Dashboard**.
2. Select **"User Management"**.
3. Click **"Add User"**.
4. Fill in the user details (Name, Email, Role).
5. The user will receive an email to set their password.

### **How do I send a system-wide announcement?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Announcements** (`/dashboard/announcements`).
2. Click **New Announcement**.
3. Enter the **Title** and **Message**.
4. Select the target audience (e.g., "All Students", "Faculty").
5. Choose the delivery method (**In-App**, **Email**, or both).
6. Click **Send**.
    *   *See [Notifications Guide](/user-guides/notifications-guide) for full details.*

### **How do I change a user's role?**
1. Navigate to **"User Management"**.
2. Search for and select the user.
3. Edit their profile and select the new **Role** from the dropdown.
4. Save changes.

### **How do I manage guest access?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Guests** (`/dashboard/guests`).
2. **View Guests**: Check the list for names, emails, roles, and statuses.
3. **Add Guest**: Click **Create Guest**, enter details, and save.
4. **Edit Guest**: Click the **Edit** icon, update details, and save.
5. **Delete Guest**: Click the **Delete** icon and confirm.

### **How do I report an issue?**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Report an Issue** (`/dashboard/report-issue`).
2. Fill in the form:
    *   **Category** — bug, feature-broken, performance, ui-ux, data-loss, security, other
    *   **Severity** — low, normal, high, critical
    *   **Title** — 5–200 characters
    *   **Description** — 10–5000 characters
3. Technical context (browser, page URL, app version) is auto-captured.
4. Click **Submit** — the report is saved and a GitHub Issue is created automatically.
5. Track your reports in the **My Reports** tab. Click **Check for Updates** to sync status from GitHub.
    *   *See [Issue Reports](/features/issue-reports) for full details.*
