## Overview

**TADA** stands for **Travel Allowance / Dearness Allowance**. It is a claim form used to request reimbursement for official travel and related expenses.

**Route**: `/dashboard/tada`

## Who Can Use TADA

- Students, professors, staff, and guests who have permission to create claims
- Administrative roles (admin, dean, librarian, superuser) can review and manage submissions

## Creating a TADA Claim

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **TADA** (`/dashboard/tada`)
2. Click **Create Claim**
3. Fill in the form sections:
   - **User Information** — name, role, registration number (auto-filled for students), and contact details
   - **Journey Details** — date of travel, purpose, mode of transport, and Metro option if applicable
   - **Expense Details** — fare, dearness allowance, and any other eligible expenses
4. Review the totals
5. Click **Save Draft** to finish later, or **Submit** to send it for processing

## Viewing Your Claims

The TADA list page shows all your claims with:

- Claim reference and date
- Journey summary
- Total amount
- Status (draft, submitted, approved, rejected, etc.)
- Actions: view, edit (if not yet finalized), print

The list is mobile-responsive and supports server-side pagination, search, and sorting.

## Printing a TADA Form

1. Open the claim you want to print
2. Click the **Print** button
3. The print layout includes:
   - Watermark for submitted/finalized claims
   - Clean sections for user information and journey details
   - Totals and signature area

## For Approvers

Administrative users can:

- Filter claims by status
- Open a claim to review details
- Approve or reject with comments
- Track claim history

## Important Notes

- Drafts can be edited until they are submitted
- Once submitted, the form is locked and cannot be edited
- Claims must be accurate; incorrect details may delay approval
