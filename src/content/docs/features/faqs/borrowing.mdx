title: "Borrowing Books"
description: "Questions about borrowing books, limits, and order requests"
---


## How do I borrow a book?

To borrow an available book, go to **Dashboard** → **Catalog** (`/dashboard/catalog`), open the book details, click **Add to Request Cart**, then open the **Request Cart** and click **Submit**. That creates a borrow request in **Dashboard** → **Orders**. When staff approve the request, the loan is created and appears in **Dashboard** → **Loans**.

If the book is unavailable, use **Reserve This Book** instead. Reservations are separate from borrow requests and place you in the queue for the next available copy.

Your available borrowing quota is shown in the catalog header. The system counts active loans, overdue loans, and pending borrow requests against that quota.

## How long does it take for my order request to be approved?

Order approval depends on library staff review. Check **Dashboard** → **Orders** for status updates, and watch your notifications for approval or rejection messages.

## Are there limits on how many books I can request?

Yes. The system checks your quota when you add books to the request cart and again when you submit requests. Active loans, overdue loans, and pending borrow requests all count toward your limit.

If your cart exceeds the remaining quota, you can submit only the allowed items and keep or move the extra items to your wishlist.

## Can I borrow reference books?

Reference books are typically for in-library use only and cannot be borrowed. These include dictionaries, encyclopedias, and other reference materials. You can use them within the library during operating hours.

## Are there restrictions on which books I can borrow?

Some books may be designated as reference materials and cannot be borrowed. These are typically available for in-library use only. Check the book's status when viewing its details.

## Can I borrow books if I have outstanding fines?

This depends on your account status. Small fines may not prevent borrowing, but significant outstanding fines or repeated violations may result in **borrowing restrictions until the fines are paid**.

## How do I search for books?

Use the search bar on the library homepage or your dashboard. You can search by:
- Title
- Author
- ISBN
- Keywords

You can also filter results by category, availability, and other criteria.

## What happens if I don't pick up my approved order?

When your order is approved, the system creates the loan automatically. Check **Orders** for request status updates and **Loans** for the resulting loan record. If a requested copy is no longer available when staff process the request, the request can be rejected and you may need to place a reservation instead.

**Tip:** Check your notifications regularly to stay updated on your order status.

## Can I transfer my borrowed books to another user?

No, borrowed books cannot be transferred to another user. Each user must borrow books under their own account. If you need to share a book, **return it first** so the other user can borrow it through their account.

## Can guests access the library system?

Yes, guests can browse the catalog and view available books, but borrowing privileges are typically limited to registered students, professors, and staff members.
